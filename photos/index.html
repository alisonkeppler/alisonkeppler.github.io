PHOTOS

As the world of Web design expands, so too do best practices and user standards. What was once an aspiration is now an expectation, and nowhere is that more apparent than with responsive design. A mobile-friendly site was formerly a "nice-to-have"; it's now non-negotiable. Understanding the details of the stratey, aesthetic and code that go into responsive sites was at the forefront of my initial education surrounding UX and front-end development, with this site as an early project to develop the skillset. Showcasing some of my photography was a means of highlighting my understanding of the need to properly display content at all stages of user interaction.
